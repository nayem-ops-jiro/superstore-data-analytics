## 📌 Business Overview & Objective
This project analyzes the **Superstore dataset** to uncover key profitability drivers, identify loss-making categories and regions, and evaluate the direct impact of high discounting strategies on overall profit margins.

---

## 💡 Key Insights & Findings
* **Discount Threshold:** Discounts exceeding **20%** directly trigger net operating losses across almost all product categories.
* **Loss Breakdown:** Uncovered over **$156K+ in total losses**, with **18.8%** of all orders operating at a negative profit margin.
* **Underperforming Products:** *Tables* and *Bookcases* account for the largest share of category-level losses.
* **Geographic Drag:** Key states such as *Texas* and *Illinois* show heavy margin erosion despite high sales volume.
